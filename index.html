📅 Conference Challenge – "30 Days from Dreams to Reality"

Before leaving the conference, every participant will complete an Action Commitment Card, committing to start a real project within 30 days. Projects may include:

  • Starting a small business.
  • Learning a digital or AI skill.
  • Launching a ministry or community outreach.
  • Beginning a savings or investment plan.
  • Completing a professional CV and applying for jobs.
  • Starting an online course or certification.
  • Developing a personal brand or portfolio.
  • Solving a community problem through a practical initiative.

Participants will be encouraged to join accountability groups and reconnect after 30 days to share progress, celebrate successes, and receive mentorship. This ensures the conference moves beyond inspiration to measurable transformation, making the theme "From Dreams to Reality" a lived experience.
